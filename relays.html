How to select a chatmail relay?

Chatmail relays are each operated by different groups and people. Visit a chatmail relay link and then tap the QR invite code there to create a chatmail address in your favorite chatmail app:

  • nine.testrun.org is the default onboarding relay for many chatmail apps

  • mehl.cloud is geared towards German speaking users

  • mailchat.pl is geared towards Polish speaking users

  • chatmail.woodpeckersnest.space and chatmail.culturanerd.it are focused on Italian speaking users

  • chat.adminforge.de is hosted in Falkenstein, Germany and is geared towards German speaking users

  • chika.aangat.lahat.computer is hosted in Santa Clara, California US

  • tarpit.fun is hosted in Nuremberg, Germany

  • d.gaufr.es is hosted in Roubaix, France

  • chtml.ca is hosted in Quebec, Canada

  • e2ee.wang is hosted in Johannesburg, South Africa

  • chat.privittytech.com is hosted in Bangalore, India

  • e2ee.im is hosted in Orastie, Romania

  • chatmail.email is hosted in Warsaw, Poland

  • chat.in-the.eu is hosted in Falkenstein, Germany

  • chat.nuvon.app is hosted in Prague, Czechia

  • nibblehole.com is hosted in Zug, Switzerland

  • chat.zashm.org is hosted in Lviv, Ukraine

  • chat.sus.fr is hosted in Iceland, Japan, Kenya, and South Africa

  • delta.thelab.uno is hosted in Gravelines, France

  • chat.vim.wtf is hosted in Frankfurt, Germany

  • uninterest.ing is hosted in Elk Grove Village, Illinois US

  • sweetfern.net is hosted in Ashburn, Virginia US

  • delta.disobey.net is hosted in Roon, Netherlands

Several additional chatmail relays are not listed publically because some countries tried to block published relays, and also because some operators prefer to do their own outreach to communities of their choice. Private or public, chatmail relays are always interoperable with each other.

For further background on chatmail relays, and how to setup one yourself, please refer to the https://chatmail.at/doc/relay documentation.
